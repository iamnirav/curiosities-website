Blurb

The Curiosities Jazz Band is a tiny ensemble playing the standards of the 1920s, ’30s, and ’40s. Led by longtime swing dancer and guitarist Nirav Sanghani, the band focuses on a classic repertoire that’s great for dancing and listening.

Biography

The Curiosities Jazz Band began in 2017 when San Francisco-based guitarist Nirav Sanghani needed a smaller version of his seven-piece swing band. Designed for restaurants, bars, and smaller stages, the Curiosities has developed into a flexible ensemble that can handle anything from an intimate dinner to a swing dance.

The core trio is comprised of acoustic guitar, tenor saxophone and upright bass, and can be expanded to include any combination of vocals, piano, and drums.

In every instrumentation, the band focuses on developing a classic repertoire for listening or dancing. Nirav draws inspiration from the influential 1930s–40s small group swing of Benny Goodman, Artie Shaw, and Count Basie, as well as contemporary groups like The EarRegulars and Mona’s Hot Four.

The Curiosities Jazz Band now plays their fun and energetic brand of traditional jazz several times a month around the San Francisco Bay Area.
